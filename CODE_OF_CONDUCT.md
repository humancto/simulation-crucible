# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want The Simulation Crucible community to be open, respectful, and useful for everyone.

Participants are expected to:

- Focus on ideas and evidence.
- Assume good intent and communicate directly.
- Give actionable, technical feedback.
- Respect differences in background and experience.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption, or bad-faith arguments.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Discriminatory or exclusionary language.

## Scope

This applies in project spaces, including issues, PRs, discussions, and community channels associated with this repo.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, report it privately to the maintainers via security/contact guidance in `SECURITY.md` or `SUPPORT.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation for violations. Actions should be proportional, documented, and focused on restoring constructive collaboration.
